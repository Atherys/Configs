Doomsayer - (Melee) doomsayer is a medium-heavy armored magic damage warrior with sustain. Doomsayer is good for fighting heavy armored classes, and putting down magic damage on tanks.

Hero Stats

Class Stats
Health Max - 250
Mana Regen - 3

Armor
Helmet - Leather, Chainmail, Iron, Diamond
Chestplate - Leather, Chainmail, Iron
Leggings - Leather, Chainmail, Iron
Boots - Leather, Chainmail, Iron

Weapons
Sword – Diamond

Trade Skills

Defences
N/A

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Repair
You have the ability to repair armor and weapons by targeting an anvil and using the skill.

Main Skills

Level 1 - Wound
Wound your target, dealing 7 physical damage every 5 seconds for 25 seconds total and negating their healing for 10 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 35s

Level 1 - Shield
When holding an Iron Door in hand and crouching, you only take .5 percent physical damage and .7 percent projectile damage. Wooden Doors and Trapdoors also provide lesser resistances.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 0s

Level 10 - Inflame
Your sword turns into fire, causing your melees to passively deal magic damage instead of physical damage and deal double damage to mobs.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 0s

Level 20 - Kick
You deal 10 physical damage to your target, knock them into the air, and silence them for 1 seconds.
Damage 10 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 20s

Level 30 - BattleCry
You deal 5 magic damage and silence enemies within 5 blocks for 1 seconds while also giving yourself and allies within 10 blocks a melee damage boost of 2 for 6 seconds.
Damage 5 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 25s

Level 40 – BurningPresence
Toggleable aura that gives you resistance to fire damage and does 8 damage every 5 seconds to all targets within 5 blocks of you.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 5s

Level 50 - LavaStride
You are able to run across lava for 10 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 30s

Level 60 - Corruption
Throw a corrupting potion that will invert healing skills of enemies within 2 blocks of the impact into a maximum of 50 damage and negate their healing skills for 5 seconds.
Damage 5 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 30s

Level 70 - Lifesteal
Passive healthgain of 7 on attack. Can be activated 3 seconds after each activation.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 3s

Level 80 - InfernalBlade
For the next 6 seconds, when you melee enemies, your melee attack damage is also dealt to all other enemies in a cone in front of you and within 7 blocks.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 25s

Level 90 - Undeath
You are saved from death for 10 seconds before dying. If you get a kill you do not die from Undeath.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 10m 

Level 100 - Condemn
Disables your targets weapon, makes you unstoppable, and deals 3 damage every 1 second for 5 seconds.
Damage 3 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 30s
