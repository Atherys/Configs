Tactician - (Melee) tactician is a medium armored support warrior with mobile buffs. Tactician is good for supporting and providing buffs his/her team 

Hero Stats

Class Stats
Health Max - 270
Mana Regen - 3

Armor
Helmet - Leather, Chainmail, Iron
Chestplate - Leather, Chainmail, Diamond
Leggings - Leather, Chainmail, Iron
Boots - Leather, Chainmail, Iron

Weapons
Axe – Diamond

Trade Skills

Defences
N/A

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Repair
You have the ability to repair armor and weapons by targeting an anvil and using the skill.

Main Skills

Level 1 – Shield
When holding an Iron Door in hand and crouching, you only take .5 percent physical damage and .7 percent projectile damage. Wooden Doors and Trapdoors also provide lesser resistances.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 0s

Level 1 - Wound
Wound your target, dealing 7 physical damage every 5 seconds for 25 seconds total and negating their healing for 10 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 35s

Level 10 - Rally
You and allies within 10 blocks recieve speed 3 for 20 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 60s

Level 20 - Kick
You deal 10 physical damage to your target, knock them into the air, and silence them for 1 seconds.
Damage 10 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 20s

Level 30 - BattleCry
You deal 5 magic damage and silence enemies within 5 blocks for 1 seconds while also giving yourself and allies within 10 blocks a melee damage boost of 2 for 6 seconds.
Damage 5 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 25s

Level 40 – BattleCommander
Target ally within 15 blocks becomes the BattleCommander. If no ally is targeted, you become the BattleCommander. Cannot be cast on a target that is already the BattleCommander or MoraleOfficer.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 2s

Level 50 - Command
Send a command, giving all allies within 7 blocks of the BattleCommander Speed 2 and .3 percent additional melee damage for 5 seconds. Also gives all allies within 7 blocks of the MoraleOfficer 5 health and 4 mana every 1 seconds for 5 seconds total.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 30s

Level 60 - MoraleOfficer
Allies within 10 blocks of the MoraleOfficer recieve 4 health and 4 mana every 2 seconds for 10 seconds. Buff repeats every 30 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 5m

Level 70 - Recover
Cure all harmful effects from allies within 10 blocks of the MoraleOfficer.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 30s

Level 80 - Track
Track a target. If the player is within 3000 blocks, you will obtain their co-ordinates, current health, class and class level.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 45s

Level 90 - ShatteringStrike
Break the Invulnerability or Bulwark effect of target enemy within 8 blocks.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 15s 

Level 100 - March
Order a March, making all allies within 8 blocks of the BattleCommander Untouchable for 5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 50s
