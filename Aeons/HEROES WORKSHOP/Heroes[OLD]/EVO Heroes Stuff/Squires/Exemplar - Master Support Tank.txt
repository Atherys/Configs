Overlord - (Melee) overlord is a medium armored support warrior, with great zone denial. Overlord is good for supporting his/her team, and peeling for the healers and damage dealers.

Trade Skills

Defences
N/A

Potion

Repair
You have the ability to repair armor and weapons by targeting an anvil and using the skill.

You only recieve 0.6 percent healing from your own healing spells.
