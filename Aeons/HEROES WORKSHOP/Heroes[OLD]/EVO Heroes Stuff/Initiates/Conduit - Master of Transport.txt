Conduit - (ranged) conduit is a light armored mage with great utility and transportation. Conduit is good for transportation and moving across atherys in a short amount of time.

Hero Stats

Class Stats
Health Max - 235
Mana Regen - 10

Armor
Helmet - Leather, Gold
Chestplate - Leather
Leggings - Leather
Boots - Leather

Weapons
Hoe - Diamond

Trade Skills

Defences
Reduction of magic damage by 25 percent.

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Repair
You are able to repair armor and weapons by targeting an anvil and using the skill.

Enchant
You have the ability to enchant armor and weapons using Enchanter's Dust.

Disenchant
Removes the enchantment from the item you are holding.

Main Skills

Damage 0 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 15s
