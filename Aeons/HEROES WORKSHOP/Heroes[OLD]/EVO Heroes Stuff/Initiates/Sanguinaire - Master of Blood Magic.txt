Sanguinaire - (Melee/ranged) Sanguinaire is a light armored mage-rogue with life steal and good crowd control. Sanguinaire excels at picking out targets and killing them.

Hero Stats

Class Stats
Health Max - 240
Mana Regen - 4

Armor
Helmet - Leather, Gold
Chestplate - Leather
Leggings - Leather
Boots - Leather

Weapons
Sword - Diamond

Trade Skills

Defences
Reduction of magic damage by 25 percent.

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Enchant
You have the ability to enchant armor and weapons using Enchanter's Dust.

Disenchant
Removes the enchantment from the item you are holding.

Main Skills

Level 1 - Fireball
Launch a fireball that deals 20 magic damage and lights your target on fire.
Damage 20 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 4s

Level 10 - Smoke
Disappear from view completely for 12 seconds. Silent to other players.
Damage 0 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 20s

Level 20 - Leech
Deal 5 magic damage to a target enemy every 4 seconds and gain 8 health every 2 seconds. Lasts 20 seconds.
Damage 5 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 25s

Level 30 - Piggify
Force the target to ride a pig for 5 seconds. While affected, they are immune to all damage until meleed or the duration expires.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 30s

Level 40 - Inversion
Deal magic damage based on how much mana your target is missing, capped at 50 magic damage total.
Damage 50 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 75s

Level 50 – Lifesteal
Gain 5 health on-hit.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 3s

Level 60 - Blink
Teleport 15 blocks in the direction you are facing.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 15s

Level 70 - BloodBoil
Stun enemies within 6 blocks for 1 seconds. Heal for 10 health for every player you stun up to 10 players.
Damage 5 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 45s

Level 80 - Confuse
Your target wanders in random directions for 5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 35s

Level 90 - Web
Place a web at target location for 3 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 35s

Level 100 - BloodBend 
Mark your target. Right click the ground within 10 seconds to teleport your target to the clicked location and root them.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 60s
