Tempest - (ranged) tempest is a light armored mage with burst magic damage. Tempest is good for bursting down targets and dropping down the back line.

Hero Stats

Class Stats
Health Max - 240
Mana Regen - 9

Armor
Helmet - Leather, Gold
Chestplate - Leather
Leggings - Leather
Boots - Leather

Weapons
Hoe - Diamond

Trade Skills

Defences
Reduction of magic damage by 25 percent.

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Enchant
You have the ability to enchant armor and weapons using Enchanter's Dust.

Disenchant
Removes the enchantment from the item you are holding.

Main Skills

Level 1 - Spark
Fire a projectile dealing 20 magic damage, and gain 1 Spark stack. Maximum 3 stacks
Damage 20 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 2s

Level 10 - ChainLightning
Strike the target with lightning, dealing 15 magic damage. Deal 15 additional damage per Spark stack. ChainLightning also arcs to nearby enemies equal to the amount of current Spark stacks, but damage decreases by 5 for each consecutive enemy struck.
Damage 15 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 15s

Level 20 - ArcanePulse
Send a pulse of energy out in a cone extending 7 blocks in front of you. The 3 closest enemies are knocked back further the closer they are to you and are slowed for 5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 25s

Level 30 - Piggify
Force the target to ride a pig for 4 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 30s

Level 40 - Inversion
Deal magic damage based on how much mana your target is missing, capped at 50 magic damage total.
Damage 50 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 75s

Level 50 - Grounded
Receive .5 percent less damage from Lightning skills.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 0s

Level 60 - BoltBlink
Teleport 10 blocks in the direction you are facing, and strike the closest enemy within 7 blocks with lighting, dealing 25 magic damage.
Damage 25 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 15s

Level 80 - Tornado
After channeling for 3 seconds, send up to 5 enemies within 10 blocks swirling upwards in a tornado for 5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 30s

Level 100 - LightningStorm
After channeling for 4 seconds, summon a storm of lightning, stunning a nearby enemy within 15 blocks for 1 seconds and doing 30 magic damage. Each enemy can only be struck once. The storm strikes every 1 second and subsides after 10 seconds.                                          
Damage 30 / Mana 20 / Cooldown 60s
