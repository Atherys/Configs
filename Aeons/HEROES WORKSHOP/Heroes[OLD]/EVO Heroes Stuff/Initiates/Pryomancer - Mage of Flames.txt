Pyromancer - (ranged) pyromancer is a light-medium armored mage with area magic damage. Pyromancer is good for dealing large amounts of area damage from a distance.

Hero Stats

Class Stats
Health Max - 230
Mana Regen - 7

Armor
Helmet - Leather, Gold
Chestplate – Leather, Gold
Leggings - Leather
Boots - Leather

Weapons
Hoe - Diamond

Trade Skills

Defences
Reduction of magic damage by 25 percent.

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Enchant
You have the ability to enchant armor and weapons using Enchanter's Dust.

Disenchant
Removes the enchantment from the item you are holding.

Main Skills

Level 1 - Fireball
Launch a fireball that deals 20 magic damage and lights your target on fire.
Damage 20 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 2s

Level 10 - Meteor
Fire a meteor in the direction you are facing, dealing 35 magic damage to enemies within 3 blocks.
Damage 35 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 10s

Level 20 - ArcanePulse
Send a pulse of energy out in a cone extending 7 blocks in front of you. The 3 closest enemies are knocked back further the closer they are to you and are slowed for 5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 25s

Level 30 - Piggify
Force the target to ride a pig for 5 seconds. While affected, they are immune to all damage until meleed or the duration expires.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 30s

Level 40 - Inversion
Deal magic damage based on how much mana your target is missing, capped at 50 magic damage total.
Damage 50 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 75s

Level 50 - BurningPresence
Toggleable aura that gives you resistance to fire damage and does 8 damage every 5 seconds to all targets within 5 blocks of you.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 5s

Level 60 - LavaStride
You are able to run across lava for 10 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 15s

Level 70 - Cauterize
Removes Bleed effect when activated.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 30s

Level 80 - FireVolley
Fire a volley of fireballs that will do 20 for every second they are in the air and also light the target on fire (Max damage 40). 
Damage 40 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 25s

Level 90 - BlazingSpeed
Gain speed 3 and light surrounding enemies on fire. 
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 25s

Level 100 - Pyroblast 
After channeling for 5 seconds, fire a Meteor every 1 second for 5 seconds total, each dealing 20 magic damage to all enemies within 3 blocks.
Damage 20 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 45s
