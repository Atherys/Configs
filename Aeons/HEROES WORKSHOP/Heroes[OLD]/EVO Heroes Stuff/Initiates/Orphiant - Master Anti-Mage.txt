Orphiant - (melee/ranged) Orphiant is a light armored mage-rogue with mana drain abilities and crowd control. Orphiant is good for taking out mana-dependent classes.

Hero Stats

Class Stats
Health Max - 240
Mana Regen - 4

Armor
Helmet - Leather, Gold
Chestplate - Leather
Leggings - Leather
Boots - Leather

Weapons
Sword – Diamond

Trade Skills

Defences
Reduction of magic damage by 25 percent.

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Enchant
You have the ability to enchant armor and weapons using Enchanter's Dust.

Disenchant
Removes the enchantment from the item you are holding.

Main Skills

Level 1 - Fireball
Launch a fireball that deals 20 magic damage and lights your target on fire.
Damage 20 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 4s

Level 10 - Smoke
Disappear from view completely for 12 seconds. Silent to other players.
Damage 0 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 20s

Level 20 - Siphon
Steal 7 mana on-hit. Steal an extra 5 mana if Absorb is active.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 3s

Level 30 - Inversion
Deal magic damage based on how much mana your target is missing, capped at 50 magic damage total.
Damage 0 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 75s

Level 40 - Blink
Teleport 15 blocks in the direction you are facing.
Damage 0 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 15s

Level 50 - EnergyShield
Gain a shield that absorbs .65 percent of incoming physical damage. The shield explodes at the end of the 10 second duration or if 100 damage is absorbed, dealing 20 damage and knocking back nearby enemies.
Damage 0 / Mana 25 / Cooldown 30s

Level 60 - Confuse
Your target wanders in random directions for 5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 35s

Level 70 - Absorb
Gain a shield that absorbs .65 percent magic damage for 10 seconds and converts the damage absorbed to mana.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 30s

Level 80 - Daze
Imbue, your next successful melee will slow and silence for 5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 20 / Cooldown 35s

Level 90 - PositionSwap
Swap positions with the target enemy.
Damage 0 / Mana 2.5 / Cooldown 15s

Level 100 - ManaBomb
Place a bomb that periodically pulls enemies within 8 blocks towards it. After 3 second duration the bomb explodes dealing 30 magic damage and draining 15 mana to all enemies within 5 blocks.
Damage 30 / Mana 35 / Cooldown 30s
