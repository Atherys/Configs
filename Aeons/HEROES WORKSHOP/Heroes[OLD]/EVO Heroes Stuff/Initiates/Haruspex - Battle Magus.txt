Haruspex - (ranged) Haruspex is a light armored mage with area damage and crowd control. Haruspex is good for locking down targets and putting out good area damage. 

Hero Stats

Class Stats
Health Max - 235
Mana Regen - 8

Armor
Helmet - Leather, Gold
Chestplate - Leather
Leggings - Leather
Boots - Leather

Weapons
Hoe - Diamond

Trade Skills

Defences
Reduction of magic damage by 25 percent.

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Enchant
You have the ability to enchant armor and weapons using Enchanter's Dust.

Disenchant
Removes the enchantment from the item you are holding.

Main Skills

Level 1 - ArcaneOrb
Fire an exploding orb that deals 30 magic damage to enemies within a 3 block radius from where it lands.
Damage 30 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 3s

Level 10 - Blink
Teleport 15 blocks in the direction you are facing.
Damage 0 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 15s

Level 20 - ArcanePulse
Send a pulse of energy out in a cone extending 7 blocks in front of you. The 3 closest enemies are knocked back further the closer they are to you and are slowed for 5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 25s

Level 30 - Piggify
Force the target to ride a pig for 5 seconds. While affected, they are immune to all damage until meleed or the duration expires.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 30s

Level 40 - Inversion
Deal magic damage based on how much mana your target is missing, capped at 50 magic damage total.
Damage 50 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 75s

Level 50 - Push
Push target enemy away from the caster.
Damage 1 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 25s

Level 60 - Web
Place a web at target location for 6 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 30s

Level 70 - Pull
Pull target enemy towards the caster.
Damage 1 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 25s

Level 80 - PositionSwap
Swap positions with the target enemy. 
Damage 0 / Mana 2.5 / Cooldown 15s

Level 90 - MassPiggify
Force all enemies within 10 blocks to ride pigs. 
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 60s

Level 100 - Vortex 
Place a vortex, and after 2 seconds, teleport enemies within 15 blocks to the vortex.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 35s
