Terramancer - (Melee-medium range cc abilities) Terramancer is a medium armored mage with great peeling and crowd control. Terramancer is a great disruption cc class, and a great class for peeling for allies.

Hero Stats

Class Stats
Health Max - 240
Mana Regen - 7

Armor
Helmet - Leather, Gold
Chestplate - Leather, Iron
Leggings - Leather, Iron
Boots - Leather, Iron

Weapons
Hoe - Diamond

Trade Skills

Defences
Reduction of magic damage by 40 percent.

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Enchant
You have the ability to enchant armor and weapons using Enchanter's Dust.

Disenchant
Removes the enchantment from the item you are holding.

Main Skills

Level 1 - Spire
Summon a spire that launches enemies within 4 blocks away.
Damage 0 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 5s

Summon up to 3 stone walls at target locations for 12 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 15s

Level 40 - Grounded

Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 0s

Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 30s
