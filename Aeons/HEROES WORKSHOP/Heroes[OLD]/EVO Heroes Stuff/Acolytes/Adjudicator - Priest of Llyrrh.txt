Adjudicator (melee) - Adjudicator is a medium armored class with good disruption capabilities. Adjudicator is a good class for backline disruption.

Hero Stats

Class Stats
Max Health - 275
Mana Regen - 35

Armor
Helmet - Leather, Chain, Diamond
Chestplate – Leather, Chain
Leggings – Leather, Chain
Boots – Leather, Chain

Weapons
Sword - Diamond

Trade Skills

Defences
Reduction of magic damage by 15 percent.

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Alchemy
You have the ability to create potions in a brewing stand. 

Main Skills

Level 1 - Wrath
You become filled with holy wrath, gaining speed and 6 additional melee damage for 8 seconds.
Damage 6 / Mana 30 / Cooldown 15s

Level 10 - Judgement
Attackers also feel 0.1 percent of melee damage done to you at the cost of 1 mana each activation.
Damage 0.1 / Mana 1 / Cooldown 0s

Level 20 - Absolution
Give 15 mana every 2 seconds for 6 seconds total to 3 party members with the lowest mana within 20 blocks of you.
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 45s

Level 30 - Repentance
Give yourself or an ally within 7 blocks with the lowest health a buff that also knocks back and damages attackers for 100 percent of their melee damage for 5 seconds or until 50 damage is done.
Damage 50 / Mana 20 / Cooldown 30s

Level 40 - Innerlight
Create a shield that will expire after 100 damage is absorbed or 15 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 40 / Cooldown 25s

Level 50 - Shatter
Destroy all items placed by other skills within 10 blocks.
Damage 0 / Mana 12.5 / Cooldown 30s

Level 60 - Hamstring
Imbue your weapon, causing your next attack to deal 5 extra damage and slow the target for 6 seconds. Expires after 7.5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 15s

Level 70 - Jump
Launch yourself several blocks forward. Silent to other players.
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 7s

Level 80 - Wrestle
Stun all enemies within 3 blocks for 1.5 seconds.
Damage 10 / Mana 35 / Cooldown 25s

Level 90 - VoidBlade
Passive 100 percent chance for melees to silence target for 1 second duration.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 5s

Level 100 - Verdict
Pass judgement on target enemy within 4 blocks, stunning them and yourself for 5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 25 / Cooldown 35s
