Rhetorician - (melee/ranged) rhetorician is a very light armored class with buffs and team support. Rhetorician is good for providing physical and magical resistance for allies and minor heals.

Defences
Reduction of magic damage by 15 percent.

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Alchemy
Grants the ability to create potions in a brewing stand.

Main Skills

Level 1 - Smite
Use holy power to smite the target for 15 magic damage. Deals double damage to undead creatures.
Damage 15 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 2s

Level 10 - Pray
Restore 50 health to yourself and a target ally within 15 blocks.
Damage 0 / Mana 7.5 / Cooldown 20s

Level 20 - Repentance
Give yourself or an ally within 7 blocks with the lowest health a buff that also knocks back and damages attackers for 1 percent of their melee damage for 5 seconds or until 50 damage is done.
Damage 50 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 30s

Level 30 - Absolution
Give 15 mana every 2 seconds for 6 seconds total to 3 party members with the lowest mana within 20 blocks of you.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 45s

Level 40 - Zeal
Buff the melee damage of a target ally by .3 percent for 10 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 20s

Level 50 – BlessedPresence
Togglable - Restore 10 health to all allies with 15 blocks every 5 seconds at the cost of 2 mana per tick.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 5s

Level 60 - Rally
You and allies within 10 blocks recieve speed 3 for 20 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 45s

Level 70 - Grace
You and allies within 10 blocks are also invulnerable when stunned.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 0s

Level 80 - Support
Give allies within 10 blocks .5 percent physical resistance and .5 percent magic resistance for 10 seconds. 
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 30s

Level 90 - Invuln
You become completely invulnerable to damage and cannot deal damage for 5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 45s

Level 100 - MassDispell 
Remove 10 debuffs from every party member within 12 blocks.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 15s
