Balladeer - (melee/ranged) balladeer is a very light armored class with debuffs and team support. Balladeer is good for providing support for their team and dropping fight changing debuffs on the enemies.

Hero Stats

Class Stats

Mana Regen - 5

Armor
Helmet - Gold
Chestplate - N/A
Leggings - N/A

Weapons

Trade Skills

Defences
Reduction of magic damage by 15 percent.

Potion
Grants the ability to use potions when out of combat.

Alchemy
Grants the ability to create potions in a brewing stand.

Main Skills

Level 1 - Smite
Use holy power to smite the target for 15 magic damage. Deals 300 percent damage to undead creatures.
Damage 15 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 2s

Level 10 - Nimble
You can dodge enemy melees, negating the damage.
Damage 0 / Mana 0 / Cooldown 2.5s

Level 20 - Pray
Restore 50 health to yourself and a target ally within 10 blocks.
Damage 0 / Mana 5 / Cooldown 20s

Level 30 - Absolution
Give 15 mana every 2 seconds for 6 seconds total to 3 party members with the lowest mana within 20 blocks of you.
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 30s

Level 40 - Seduce
Remove the armor of a target enemy for 5 seconds, you saucy devil.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 25s

Level 50 – Minuet
Tether two enemies together for 6 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 15s

Level 60 - Invuln
You become Invulnerable to damage and cannot deal damage for 5 seconds. Can be cast while silenced.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 45s

Level 70 - Ballad
Disarm all enemies within 5 blocks, putting their weapon into their inventory.
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 15s

Level 80 - Preach
Channel for 3 seconds, then stun all enemies within 10 blocks for 2.5 seconds.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 30s

Level 90 - PeacefulHymn
Place a jukebox. Every 4 seconds for 20 seconds total, the melee damage of enemies within 12 blocks is reduced by 5 for 4 seconds. 
Damage 0 / Mana 15 / Cooldown 45s

Level 100 - Prelude 
Channel for 10 seconds.  While channeling, reduce the healing spells of enemies within 13 blocks by .8 percent.
Damage 0 / Mana 10 / Cooldown 30s
